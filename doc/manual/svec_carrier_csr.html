  • fmc0_pres [ro]: FMC 1 presence
    0: FMC slot 1 is populated
    1: FMC slot 1 is not populated.
  • fmc1_pres [ro]: FMC 2 presence
    0: FMC slot 2 is populated
    1: FMC slot 2 is not populated.
  • sys_pll_lck [ro]: System clock PLL status
    0: not locked
    1: locked.
  • ddr0_cal_done [ro]: DDR3 bank 4 calibration status
    0: not done
    1: done.
  • ddr1_cal_done [ro]: DDR3 bank 5 calibration status
    0: not done
    1: done.
